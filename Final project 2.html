About Us

Learn more about the King University Alumni Association

The King University Alumni Association is a community of graduates who are dedicated to supporting the mission and vision of King University. Our goal is to foster lifelong relationships between alumni and the university, and to provide opportunities for alumni to connect with each other and with current students.

As a member of the Alumni Association, you'll have access to a variety of benefits and resources, including:

  • Invitations to exclusive events and networking opportunities
  • Access to career services and job postings
  • Discounts on university merchandise and services
  • The ability to give back to your alma mater through volunteer opportunities and donations

Become a member today and join a vibrant community of Tornadoes!
